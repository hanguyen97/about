Ha Nguyen

Hello! I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics at Cornell University in Ithaca, New York. My research interests are machine learning and high dimensional statistics.

Prior to Cornell, I worked on health economics and outcomes research and developed R software at Analysis Group. I graduated with a BA in Mathematics and Psychology from St Olaf College in 2019.
